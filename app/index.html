Water. It affects us all.

Is at the heart of a daily crisis faced by countless millions of the world’s most vulnerable people. People whose livelihoods and lives are threathened on a devastating scale.

Raising Awareness.

This site is aimed to raise awareness on the global water crisis and how it affects human development. We also aim to inform users on how best to conserve water through the water score test. The more people aware of this crisis the greater the likelihood it can be solved.

Solving the crisis. Playing your part.

Everyone can contribute in some small way to help solve this water crisis. Whether by making a conscious effort to conserve water or donating to charities. We can all play a part in mitigating the severe issues millions of humans face daily.

About Us

H2O & You

H2O & You – from home to others – is a non-profit organization founded in 2009, aimed at raising awareness of the water scarcity problem in the world across places like Sub-Saharan Africa, Asia and the Middle East.

We seek to help many of those who cannot help themselves out of their current situations. We work hand in hand with our sister organization – Drops Away From Drought, within Trinidad and Tobago and the wider Caribbean.

Our mission is to help out as much as we can but more importantly change the mindset of our younger and future generations.

H2O Young Hands – H2OYH, is the youth branch of our organization - created in 2011 when there was an overwhelming response by children interested in getting involved with our projects. The work of H2OYH takes on a hands-on and more interactive approach with the projects geared towards widening the scope of thought and widening the horizons of our children.

As Mahatma Gandhi rightly said “You must be the Change you want to see in the world.”
